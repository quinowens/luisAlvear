Code For Good Hackathon: October 7-8, 2022


Code For Good is a hackathon organized by JPMorgan Chase where university students from all over the world are invited to use their skills to help a nonprofit organization further their mission.
Students are given 24 hours to develop a solution to the problem presented by the organizations. My team and I were tasked with building a learning management system to help Refined and Refreshed Inc. break the cycle of generational poverty. We were in a team of 5 students and over the 24 hours, we developed a web application that would allow the organization to track the progress of their students and provide them with the resources they need to succeed. It was an amazing experience and I am so grateful to have been a part of it. I got to work in a team of likeminded individuals, all with different skillsets and backgrounds, and we were assisted by mentors from JPMorgan Chase. I learned a lot about teamwork and how to work in a fast-paced environment. By the end of the event, we were able to code up a final product and get to see our work deployed into the real world.
